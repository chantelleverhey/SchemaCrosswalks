About this page

This page was created in support of the outputs from the Research Data Alliance Research Metadata Schemas Working Group. Specifically, a crosswalk of schema.org properties and corresponding metadata terms. The original crosswalk was created by (insert student name) at Monash University in late 2019. The tables below represent the crosswalk when it was ingested into the site in early January 2020 and is not a live read of the original table. The page is intended as a prototype service for the research data management community in support of managers who are investigating options for including schema.org markup into existing well formed metadata.

This table is a free text search over both metadata terms and schema.org properties. Wildcard searches are not supported but partial searches are.
